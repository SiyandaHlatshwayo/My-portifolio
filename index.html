About Me

My name is Siyanda Hlatshwayo, and I am a Hydrology and Geography graduate with a Bachelor of Science degree. I work mainly with Geographic Information Systems (GIS), remote sensing and water resource management. I use tools like Google Earth Engine, ArcGIS Pro, QGIS and Python to study and solve environmental problems. I also have skills in web development, where I create clean, responsive and modern websites. My goal is to use both my technical and analytical skills to understand, monitor and protect our water and environmental systems, helping to build sustainable and data-driven solutions for the future
